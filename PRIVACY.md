# Privacy Policy (v1.0)

**Effective Date:** December 2024  
**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Overview

MarketMind is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our e-commerce automation platform.

## Information We Collect

### Data Types
- **Account Information:** Username, email address, contact details
- **Operational Logs:** System performance, error logs, usage analytics
- **Product Data:** E-commerce product information you connect to our platform
- **Business Data:** Sales data, pricing information, inventory levels
- **Technical Data:** IP addresses, browser information, device identifiers

### How We Collect Data
- **Directly from you:** When you register, configure integrations, or contact support
- **Automatically:** Through your use of our platform and connected services
- **From third parties:** Via authorized integrations with e-commerce platforms

## How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:
- **Operate the service:** Process your requests, manage your account, provide platform functionality
- **Provide support:** Respond to inquiries, troubleshoot issues, provide technical assistance
- **Ensure security:** Detect fraud, prevent abuse, maintain platform security
- **Improve our service:** Analyze usage patterns, develop new features, optimize performance
- **Legal compliance:** Meet regulatory requirements, respond to legal requests

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:
- **With your consent:** When you explicitly authorize data sharing
- **Service providers:** Third-party vendors who assist in platform operations (see Subprocessors)
- **Legal requirements:** When required by law, court order, or government request
- **Business transfers:** In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
- **Safety and security:** To protect rights, property, or safety of users or others

## Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after closure
- **Operational logs:** Retained for 12 months for security and performance analysis
- **Business data:** Retained according to your data retention settings (default: 24 months)
- **Legal holds:** Data may be retained longer when required by law or legal proceedings

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption:** Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access controls:** Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
- **Monitoring:** Continuous security monitoring and incident response
- **Auditing:** Regular security audits and penetration testing
- **Compliance:** SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 aligned practices

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
- **Portability:** Export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your data
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, contact: **privacy@marketmind.ai**

## International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through:
- **Standard Contractual Clauses:** EU-approved data transfer mechanisms
- **Adequacy decisions:** Transfers to countries with adequate data protection
- **Binding corporate rules:** Internal policies ensuring consistent protection

## Subprocessors

We work with carefully vetted third-party service providers:
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** AWS, Google Cloud Platform
- **Monitoring:** Sentry, Datadog
- **Communications:** SendGrid, Twilio
- **Analytics:** Internal analytics only (no third-party tracking)

For a complete list, visit: **https://marketmind.ai/legal/subprocessors**

## Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:
- **Authentication:** Maintaining your login session
- **Security:** Preventing cross-site request forgery
- **Functionality:** Remembering your preferences and settings

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

MarketMind is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:
- **Notify you:** Email notification of material changes
- **Version control:** Maintain previous versions with effective dates
- **Consent:** Obtain consent for material changes that affect your rights
