What MarketMind is built to do

The goal is not a random Shopify store. The goal is a reusable commerce operator that can test multiple niches until one proves profitable, while keeping Aidan as the owner/operator and Codex as the code maintainer.

Research Engine

Tracks niches, competitors, suppliers, reviews, community pain points, and product trends with evidence and confidence labels.

Product & Offer Scoring

Scores items by margin, shipping difficulty, return risk, content potential, supplier quality, and break-even CAC.

Codex Store Specs

Generates product pages, landing pages, checkout plans, offer bundles, QA requirements, and build tasks for Codex.

P&L Monitoring

Tracks revenue, fees, fulfillment, ad spend, refunds, CAC, contribution profit, and kill/scale rules.

Approval Queue

Prepares high-risk actions for human approval, including ad launches, price changes, product publishing, supplier messages, and refunds.

Learning Loop

Reviews outcomes, writes lessons, updates scorecards, and improves future experiments instead of repeating losing patterns.

The bot works. Aidan approves risk.

MarketMind should automate work, not judgment. The model recommends actions; the runtime validates them; the host executes only what is allowed.

Allowed automatically

  • Research niches and products
  • Normalize supplier and marketplace files
  • Score products and calculate margins
  • Draft product pages and content briefs
  • Import read-only analytics
  • Create internal Codex tasks
  • Generate daily reports and lessons

Requires approval

  • Publish products or store changes
  • Launch or increase ad spend
  • Change live pricing or policies
  • Order inventory or samples
  • Send customer or supplier messages
  • Issue refunds
  • Access credentials or payment settings
